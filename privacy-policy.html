Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2025  |  Last Updated: March 2025

Global Home Health Care LLC ("Company," "we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting the privacy of our patients, employees, and website visitors. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information.

HIPAA Compliance

As a home health care provider, we are subject to the Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act (HIPAA). All patient health information (PHI) is handled in strict compliance with HIPAA Privacy and Security Rules. We maintain a separate Notice of Privacy Practices for patients, available upon request.

Information We Collect

  • Personal Identification: Name, address, phone number, email address
  • Employment Information: Resume, credentials, references, employment history (for job applicants)
  • Clinical Information: Diagnosis, insurance, care needs (for patient referrals — handled under HIPAA)
  • Website Usage: IP address, browser type, pages visited (collected via cookies)

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide, coordinate, and improve home health care services
  • To process employment applications and contact candidates
  • To follow up on patient referrals and coordinate care
  • To respond to inquiries submitted through our contact form
  • To comply with legal and regulatory obligations

Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share information with healthcare providers and insurers as necessary to provide care (under HIPAA authorization), service providers under confidentiality agreements, and government authorities as required by law.

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no internet transmission is 100% secure.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, or delete your personal information. Patients have additional rights under HIPAA. To exercise any of these rights, contact us at info@global-hhc.com.
